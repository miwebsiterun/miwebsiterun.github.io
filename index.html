    About us

    After acquiring a rich experience of over 25 years in various sectors like minerals, plastics, rubber, paint and other allied industries, Mohan Infinity was founded by Mr. Amit Kumar Golcha B.E. (Chemical) from IIT, with the following objectives for the polymer and rubber industry

  • To create new innovations
  • To offer better products at reasonable prices
  • To provide technical assistance to small and medium scale industries
  • To manufacture REACH and ROHS compliant materials to compete in the world market

    • Mohan Infinity has eminent technical consultants and experts on board to support various research works and new developments, with a special focus on nano technology and polymer alloys. Relentless hard work with a customer focused approach has helped Mohan Infinity to provide many new innovative solutions to its customers.

    Today the company offers the following quality materials for the industry

  • Functional Polymers
  • Additives
  • Micronized Calcium Carbonate from Vietnam, Malaysia, Egypt.
  • Flow additives/modifiers
  • Crosslinking enhancers
  • PH Stabilizers
  • Highly resilient polymeric alloys
